DIY Guitar

The summer before I started college my high school physics teacher invited me to come in a couple days per week so that we could each build guitars together. We took a unique approach, designing the body in CAD and then using a laser cutter to slice sections of quarter inch slabs of different woods. I wired up the pickups, which eventually led to me choosing EE as my major!
